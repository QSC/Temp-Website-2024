What is QSC?

The Queen's Space Conference (QSC) is an annual space conference held in Kingston, Ontario. The conference is centered around a series of exciting talks, innovative workshops, and networking opportunities. We strive to bring together industry professionals in order to provide delegates the most informative, inspiring, and memorable conference possible. QSC is about sparking thought and idea-generation, raising awareness of the space industry, and piquing the interest in space for the future generation.

Come to QSC 2024 to get inspired, learn, and to connect with professionals and other students!

Speakers

Every year, we bring in industry professionals to give exciting talks and presentations to our delegates. Past speaker professions include astronauts, CEOs, engineers, AI specialists, PhD astrophysicists, analysts, professors, and many more interesting and relevant positions.

Presentations are easily digestible, even if you do not have background in the relevant field! There is built-in time to connect and speak further with the speakers after presentations; past delegates have said these conversations can be quite inspiring and interesting!

More in-depth information about past speakers will be posted on this website in the coming month. Speakers for the 2024 Queen's Space Conference have yet to be finalized, so keep your eyes on the website and our social medias for updated information.
